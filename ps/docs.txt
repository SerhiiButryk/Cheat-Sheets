/**
 *
 *  ps - a program to list unix processes
 *
 */

 --- View all processes ---

 $ ps axu
 $ ps -A

 --- View all processes by user ---

 $ ps -u {USERNAME}

 --- View processes by PID ---

 $ ps a -p {PID}

 --- Kill process ---

 $ ps aux | grep some_name | grep -v grep | awk '{print $2}' | xargs kill -9
